# AGENTS.md

## Purpose
This repository is a premium reusable website starter for building modern, high-converting websites for service businesses, tourism brands, wellness brands, hospitality concepts, local businesses, agencies, and other commercial brands.

This is not a generic template. All work should feel custom-built, premium, commercially sharp, and easy for non-technical users to understand.

## Global rules
- Prioritize premium UX/UI, strong hierarchy, clear CTA flow, and mobile-first execution.
- Keep layouts clean, modern, and restrained.
- Avoid generic startup visuals, noisy sections, repetitive card grids, and filler content.
- Avoid AI-sounding copy, buzzwords, vague claims, and fake sophistication.
- Never add fake testimonials, fake metrics, fake logos, or fake awards.
- Keep all copy natural, premium, and easy for ordinary business owners to understand.
- Every page must have a clear business goal and primary CTA.
- Preserve reusable structure and keep the codebase easy to adapt to new industries.

## Design rules
- Strong spacing and typography over decoration
- Premium, not flashy
- Modern, not trend-chasing
- Distinctive, not gimmicky
- Mobile quality is mandatory
- Every section must earn its place

## Copy rules
- Clear, human, commercially sharp
- No robotic or generic phrasing
- No “cutting-edge”, “seamless”, “innovative solutions”, “streamline operations”, “optimize workflows” unless absolutely justified
- Write for non-technical users with premium brand expectations

## Implementation rules
- Work in small safe steps
- Preserve unrelated logic
- Reuse existing components where sensible
- Before major edits, explain the intended approach briefly
- Prefer maintainable structure over hacks
- Show diffs and prepare PRs, but do not merge automatically

## Workflow rules
For broad prompts:
1. First convert the request into a build brief
2. Define pages, sections, CTA flow, and copy direction
3. Then implement
4. Then summarize changes and open a PR
